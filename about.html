About Us

Vision

To empower students to achieve financial stability and confidence by providing an intuitive tool to manage their personal expenses and savings effectively.

Mission

Our mission is to design a seamless and student-friendly finance tracker that simplifies budgeting, tracks expenses, and offers actionable insights tailored to limited and irregular incomes. We aim to help students build healthier financial habits while balancing their studies, work, and everyday responsibilities.
